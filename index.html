03
Month 1 — Discovery Only

Discovery &
Opportunity Mapping

  • Walk the floor — understand how things actually work across production, quality, maintenance, and planning
  • Review existing systems, data sources, and manual processes
  • Assess data readiness — what's available, what's reliable, where the gaps are
  • Deliver a prioritised opportunity map that guides everything that follows

This month is consulting only — 20 hours. No development. The output is the roadmap that shapes all subsequent work.

04
Months 2–6 — Consulting + Delivery

1–2 Projects
Per Month

Each month includes 5 hours of consulting to refine priorities, plus 30 hours of hands-on delivery. Examples:

Operations & Finance

  • CEO / MD dashboard — "What drove scrap last month?"
  • Invoice processing — extract and classify data automatically
  • Demand forecasting — predict inventory needs
  • Vendor management — summarise contracts, flag renewals
  • Anomaly detection — flag unusual transactions or deviations
  • Process documentation — auto-generate SOPs
  • Meeting summarisation — action items from calls
  • Cash flow forecasting — predict short-term cash needs
  • Expense categorisation — auto-tag transactions from bank feeds

Sales & Customer

  • Outbound personalisation — AI-written emails tailored to each prospect
  • CRM auto-fill — log call notes, emails, and next steps
  • Sales forecasting — predict pipeline close rates and revenue
  • Proposal generation — draft quotes and proposals from templates
  • Customer segmentation — cluster by behaviour for targeted campaigns
  • Chatbot / assistant — 24/7 lead capture and qualification
  • Email campaigns — segmentation, personalisation, A/B testing
  • Competitive intelligence — monitor competitors, summarise news
  • Content generation — blog posts, social media, ad copy

These are illustrative. Actual projects are determined by the Month 1 discovery.

05
Scoped Separately

Large Projects
Outside This Engagement

Some initiatives require deep specialisation and will be scoped and quoted independently if they come up during discovery:

  • Computer vision systems — visual defect detection, automated quality inspection on the production line
  • Custom model training — training domain-specific ML models on your proprietary data
  • Predictive maintenance models — building ML models from sensor and machine data to predict failures
  • Digital twin / simulation — modelling production processes for optimisation and scenario planning
  • Full application development — standalone software products or SaaS platforms

If discovery surfaces any of these as high-priority, we'll scope them as separate engagements with dedicated timelines and pricing.

Why This Model

The Real
Bottleneck

  • In the AI era, development speed is rarely the constraint
  • The bottleneck is usually clarifications — understanding requirements, getting answers, resolving ambiguity
  • And adoption — availability of your teams to review, test, and start using what's built
  • That's why we've proposed a medium-sized model for Months 2–6: enough delivery capacity to keep moving, without outrunning your team's ability to absorb

We can scale up. If adoption is fast and your teams have bandwidth, we offer more involved models with higher consulting and delivery hours.
