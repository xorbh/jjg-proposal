05
How We Measure

Success
Criteria

Every project has a clear before-and-after. We define these metrics together during scoping and measure against them on delivery:

Efficiency

  • Time saved — % reduction in time spent on the task (e.g. invoice processing from 2 hrs → 10 min)
  • Manual steps eliminated — number of handoffs, copy-paste actions, or approvals removed
  • Response time — how fast information is available (e.g. report generation from days → seconds)

Quality

  • Accuracy improved — % reduction in errors, missed items, or incorrect data
  • Coverage — % of cases handled automatically vs. needing manual intervention
  • Adoption rate — % of target team actively using the solution after 2 weeks

Specific targets are set per project. Typical AI automations deliver 60–90% time savings and 30–50% error reduction on the processes they cover.
